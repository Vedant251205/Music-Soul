FAQ

Questions, answered

Is my Spotify data safe?

Timbre signs you in with Spotify's secure OAuth (we never see your password). To write your persona, an anonymous summary of your taste — your top genres and audio traits, not your name or login — is sent to an AI. Your Spotify data itself isn't stored.

Do I need a paid Spotify account?

No. A free Spotify account works fine — we only read your top artists, tracks, and listening history.

Can I try it without logging in?

Absolutely. Click View Demo to explore a full sample profile before connecting your account.

How is my archetype decided?

We average the audio features of your top tracks — energy, valence, danceability, and more — then match that signature to one of six archetypes.
